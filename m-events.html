Member Events

Member Events Programming

CASA accomplishes its mission of developing leadership and professional skills of current students through its Member Events Programming. This involves various workshops, from resume development to networking and leadership skills development, to the Big Bear/Little Bear Program that pairs upperclassmen with lowerclassmen in a mentorship program. CASA also participates in various community service activities in the Berkeley area, and has socials throughout the semester to encourage members to learn more about each other.
